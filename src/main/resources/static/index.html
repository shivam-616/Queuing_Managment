How It Works

A simple 3-step process for you and your visitors.

Step 1

Scan to Join

A visitor scans the QR code at your entrance, fills out the required details, and instantly gets their place in the queue.

Step 2

Wait Remotely

The visitor can wait outside, in their car, or nearby, watching their position update in real-time on their phone.

Step 3

Get Notified

The admin calls the next person from a simple web interface, and the visitor receives a notification that it's their turn.
